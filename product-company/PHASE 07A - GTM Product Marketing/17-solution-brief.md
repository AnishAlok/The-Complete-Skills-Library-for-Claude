# solution-brief.md

> **Skill:** Produces a solution brief for a specific industry, vertical, or use case that frames the product's value in the language and context of that segment.

---

## TLDR

A solution brief is a targeted version of a one-pager written for a specific industry, vertical, or use case. Where a one-pager communicates general product value, a solution brief speaks the language of a specific segment: their industry terminology, their specific regulations or workflows, their KPIs, their buyer titles. Used in vertical marketing, account-based marketing, and industry event campaigns.

---

## Topic Explanation

### Why Vertical Messaging Outperforms Generic Messaging

A VP of Operations at a healthcare company and a VP of Operations at a manufacturing company have different problems, use different vocabulary, and measure success by different metrics. A solution brief using healthcare-specific language, HIPAA compliance framing, and healthcare customer proof will resonate significantly more than a generic product brief for either audience.

The effort to adapt messaging for a vertical is modest. The impact on conversion rate within that vertical is typically substantial. Solution briefs are among the highest-ROI marketing assets for products serving multiple industries.

### Depth of Customization

Tier 1 (messaging adaptation): reframe the headline, opening, and proof points in industry language. Same product, different frame. 2 to 4 hours of work.

Tier 2 (use-case specific): built around a specific workflow within the industry. May highlight different features. One day of work.

Tier 3 (full vertical): comprehensive content for a strategic vertical with dedicated landing page, full content suite, and sales enablement. Multi-week investment.

## Output Format

```
SOLUTION BRIEF

Industry/Vertical: [specific industry]
Use case: [the workflow or scenario being addressed]

HEADLINE
[Industry-specific problem or outcome. Uses industry vocabulary.
A practitioner in this field should immediately recognize their situation.]

INDUSTRY CONTEXT
[2 to 3 sentences: what is happening in this industry that makes this problem urgent now.
Reference industry-specific trends, regulations, or market pressures.]

THE [INDUSTRY] CHALLENGE
[3 to 5 sentences describing the specific problem as this industry experiences it.
Use industry-specific vocabulary, process names, and regulatory references where applicable.
Reference the specific workflows, handoffs, or compliance requirements relevant here.]

HOW [PRODUCT] SOLVES IT FOR [INDUSTRY]
[3 to 5 sentences connecting product capabilities to specific industry workflows.
Not the generic product description: the industry-contextualized version.]

CAPABILITIES THAT MATTER FOR [INDUSTRY] (3 maximum)
[Industry-specific label]: [Benefit framed in this industry's language]
[Industry-specific label]: [Benefit]
[Industry-specific label]: [Benefit]

PROOF FROM [INDUSTRY]
"[Quote from a customer in this specific industry]"
[Full Name], [Title], [Company], [Industry sub-segment]
[OR: industry-specific metric: "Healthcare customers reduce audit prep time by X%"]

COMPLIANCE AND CERTIFICATIONS
[If relevant: applicable standards such as HIPAA / SOC2 / ISO 27001 / GDPR / FedRAMP]

HOW TO GET STARTED
[2 to 3 sentences framing the entry point in this industry's buying context]
[CTA: action framed for this industry's typical buyer]

ABOUT [COMPANY]
[1 to 2 sentence boilerplate, with industry customer count if notable]
```

## Starter Prompt

```
You are a product marketer writing a solution brief for a specific vertical.

Industry/vertical: [specific industry]
Target buyer in this industry: [title and their primary concerns]
Industry-specific problem: [the pain in this industry's own language]
Industry context: [trends, regulations, or pressures making this urgent]
Relevant product capabilities: [which features matter most for this vertical]
Industry-specific proof: [customer from this industry and their results]
Compliance or certifications relevant: [industry-specific requirements met]

Write a complete solution brief using industry-specific language throughout.
The headline must be immediately recognizable to a practitioner in this industry.
All proof must come from this specific industry.
```
